Design that honors the text.

Books, Bibles, devotionals, curriculum — these are materials people carry, mark, and return to. The design has to be invisible enough to get out of the way of the content, and strong enough to make it feel worth keeping. We work at the intersection of editorial craft and Christian publishing.

With experience designing for e625 and multiple US publishers, we understand print specifications, production timelines, and what it takes to deliver files that are ready to run — across formats, runs, and languages.
