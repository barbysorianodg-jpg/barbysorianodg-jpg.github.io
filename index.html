Who I am
Your photo
3 : 4 ratio recommended

Identity for faith
communities.

I'm Barbara Soriano, a brand and graphic designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, with over 15 years of experience helping churches and Christian organizations communicate with clarity through branding, editorial, print, and digital design.

My design journey began in my local church, where I discovered how thoughtful design can support ministry. Since then, I've worked with churches across the United States and Latin America, creating sermon series, event identities, books, discipleship resources, membership guides, and complete church rebrands.

For three years I was part of the Church Media Squad team, producing weekly creative content for U.S. churches. I've also worked with e625 for more than a decade, designing books, Bibles, curricula, and digital resources for ministries in over 20 countries. I don't just design for churches—I understand how they work.

How we work together

A clear process,
from start to finish.

01

Discovery

We start with a call — I want to understand your organization, your audience, and what you need to communicate. You'll receive a short questionnaire before we talk, so we make the most of our time together.

02

Design

Based on what I learn, I develop the work and present it with clear rationale. We refine together — two rounds of revisions are included in every project.

03

Delivery

You receive all final files — print-ready, screen-optimized, and organized so your whole team can use them. Brand projects include a usage guide your staff can follow without a designer in the room.
